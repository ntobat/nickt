My name is Nick Tobat. I grew up in Northern Virginia but right now I'm growing up in Adelaide, Australia. I started teaching myself to code in the summer of 2014, just after graduating frivolously with a degree in political science and moving to the technology Mecca that is San Francisco. It was there that I also began creating space art with spraypaint and discarded scraps of drywall, and built my very own fully functional aquaponics garden out of a plastic 55-gallon drum. What else do you want to know? I wear glasses, and I used to wear earrings in both ears. I let my facial hair grow out for the first time this year, and I gotta say I quite like it.

Anyway, keep scrolling so that you may behold the timeline of my short yet glorious life.
